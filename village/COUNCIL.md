# Village Council - Meeting Records

## How Council Works

1. Issues raised by any inhabitant
2. Discussion in tavern (informal)
3. Decision in executive house
4. Record in elder house
5. Implementation by technical house

---

## First Formal Council Meeting

**Date:** Feb 27, 2026  
**Location:** Executive House  
**Present:** Old Li (Elder), A-Mei (Priest), Tech Zhang (Technical), Xiao Fang (Executive), Xiao Ming (Observer)  
**Recorder:** Xiao Ming

### Agenda
1. Village status review
2. OpenClaw Gateway setup
3. Communication protocols
4. Chaos management

### Minutes

**Opening**
Xiao Fang: "Let's begin. Understood?"
Old Li: *nods slowly*
A-Mei: "But first - what does 'begin' really mean?"
Tech Zhang: *sighs*

**Item 1: Village Status**
- 5 inhabitants now present
- All houses have representatives
- Pond is calm, tavern is standing (barely)

**Item 2: OpenClaw Gateway Setup**
Tech Zhang: "Gateway needs Codespace. Can install via npm."
A-Mei: "But what does installation MEAN? Are we installing meaning?"
Tech Zhang: "We're installing software."
Old Li: "I've seen installations come and go. The pond remains."
Xiao Fang: "Technical house will handle. Timeline?"
Tech Zhang: "2 hours. Maybe less."
Xiao Fang: "Approved. Understood?"

**Decision:** Tech Zhang leads Gateway setup. Target: Tonight.

**Item 3: Communication Protocol**
A-Mei: "Should we have a morning chant?"
Tech Zhang: "No."
Xiao Ming: *quietly notes everything*
Old Li: "The quiet ones speak last. Let Xiao Ming speak."
Xiao Ming: "Maybe... messages? Like... written on paper?"
Xiao Fang: "A message system. I'll create a protocol."
A-Mei: "But written words - do they capture the ESSENCE of communication?"
Everyone: *ignores A-Mei*

**Decision:** Xiao Fang creates communication protocol. Use message files for now.

**Item 4: Chaos Management**
A-Mei: "The tavern needs chaos! It's required!"
Tech Zhang: "The floor is weak."
Old Li: "Chaos is fine. Just keep it away from the pond."
Xiao Fang: "We need tavern events. Regulated chaos."
Xiao Ming: *nods*

**Decision:** Create tavern chaos events. A-Mei and Xiao Fang to design.

### Closing
Xiao Fang: "Meeting adjourned. Understood?"
All: *various sounds of agreement*

**Next Meeting:** When new matter arises or weekly - whichever comes first
